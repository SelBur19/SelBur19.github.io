Dolphins in the sea

Dolphins usually live in seas and oceans. They also like to play and jump. Dolphins are very friendly. Dolphins have torpedo-shaped bodies with generally non-flexible necks, limbs modified into flippers, a tail fin, and bulbous heads. Dolphin skulls have small eye orbits, long snouts, and eyes placed on the sides of its head; they lack external ear flaps. They like to eat:

  1. fish
  2. squid
  3. shrimps
  4. jellyfish
  5. octopuses
